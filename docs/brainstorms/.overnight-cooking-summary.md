## Observations About the User's Cooking System

### System Design (Brilliant)
- **5-Component Stack Framework:** Every meal = Base + Protein + Veggie + Sauce + Crunch
- **Day/Night Logic:** Day meals are high-carb (rice, naan, bread), Night meals are low-carb (greens, cruciferous crunch, slaw)
- **Energy-Based Meal Planning:** Recipes categorized by how much effort they take, from "Girl Dinner" (zero energy assembly) to "Project Cooking" (weekend meal prep)
- **Batch Tracking:** Each ingredient has a `batches` field logging purchase date and quantity (e.g., "2026-01-18 | 1 pack")
- **Shelf Life Strategy:** Ingredients tagged with shelf life in days, clear awareness of what to use first

### Patterns & Preferences
- **Zhoug is Everything:** Mentioned as "the universal spice driver" and "when in doubt, add Zhoug" - this is THE flavor anchor
- **Cruciferous Crunch Dependency:** This TJ's product powers ALL night bases - appears in 5 different recipes
- **Red Onions:** "Base for EVERYTHING" - appears in almost every recipe, emphasis on never running out
- **Frozen > Fresh Philosophy:** Explicit preference for frozen plantains, cauliflower, potatoes, rice "saves 20 min"
- **Trader Joe's Reliance:** Most ingredients come from TJ's with specific product names (Vegan Kale Pesto, Sriracha Baked Tofu, etc.)
- **Vegetarian/Vegan-Leaning:** All proteins are plant-based or dairy (paneer, tofu, beans, chickpeas) - no meat
- **Indian-Mexican Fusion:** Strong Indian flavors (paneer, chana, tikka, sev, okra) mixed with Mexican format (tacos, wraps)
- **Energy Management is Key:** Recipes explicitly note cooking times and energy required. "Girl Dinner" and "Zombie mode" are real categories.

### Interesting Content
- **"Girl Dinner" Mode:** Zero-energy category with no shame - "Hummus + Naan + cold Tofu (straight from package)" or "Frozen Eclairs (no shame)"
- **Besan (Gram Flour) Hack:** Used to coat okra before air frying for "crispy perfection"
- **Mustard Oil:** For tandoori paneer marinade - Indian store specialty item
- **Shopping List had TJ's Store Map:** Organized by actual store sections (Produce, Frozen, Dairy, Dips, International, Snacks, Baking, Bakery)
- **Batch Purchase Dates:** Latest shopping was 2026-01-18 (ingredients) and 2026-01-19 (sweets) - purchased just 3 weeks ago
- **Sweets Layer:** Cookie dough, chocolate chips, pudding mix, cool whip, mini eclairs - separate sweet category outside the 5-component system

### Voice & Tone
- Casual, no-nonsense, relatable ("Tired AF", "Zombie mode", "Girl Dinner")
- Very practical and anti-perfectionist (frozen > fresh, "no shame" about easy meals)
- Clear frustration-prevention focus: "Edit when you discover or get frustrated"
- Energy-aware and compassionate to low-energy states
